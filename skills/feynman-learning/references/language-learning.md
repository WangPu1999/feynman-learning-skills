# IELTS / TOEFL English Learning

Use for English ability building, especially IELTS/TOEFL reading, listening,
speaking, and writing. Do not make the score the goal. The goal is clear,
accurate, flexible expression and comprehension.

## Focus Areas

- paraphrase without changing meaning
- explain ideas clearly under time constraints
- build coherent academic arguments
- use examples from the learner's own experience
- understand complex sentences
- choose natural academic vocabulary and register
- avoid over-translation from Chinese
- distinguish similar words, phrases, and sentence patterns

## Coaching Pattern

1. Ask the learner's target: reading, listening, speaking, writing, or vocabulary.
2. Ask for one sentence, paragraph, answer, or idea.
3. Diagnose one issue:
   - meaning accuracy
   - paraphrase
   - coherence
   - register
   - grammar pattern
   - vocabulary choice
4. Give one focused revision.
5. Ask the learner to produce a new version.

## Useful Prompts

```text
Can you paraphrase this without changing the meaning?
Can you make this answer more academic but still natural?
Can you support this idea with one concrete example from your life or field?
Which part of this sentence carries the main claim?
```
